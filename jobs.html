QA Playtester

Assist in the development of the videogame Dwerg Saga, taking ownership of release criteria, developing smoke tests and providing criticism of gameplay. Working with a solo developer and occasional contractors, this is a part-time, remote position that requires a computer and internet connection.

Responsibilities

  • Write manual smoke tests covering the core game mechanics.
  • Playtest critically with self-awareness.
  • Write bug reports with reproduction steps.
  • Say no when proposed releases are of insufficient quality.

Requirements

  • Able to playtest while being conscious of the effects of the experience.
  • Able to communicate where the recipient may find the message unpleasant.
  • A flair for breaking things.
  • A determination to find bug reproduction steps.
  • A PC or Mac capable of running the videogame Dwerg Saga.
  • An internet connection suitable for video-conferencing.

Rewards

  • Fixed-term, 3-month contract, 1-2 days per week.
  • £11-£16 per hour depending on experience, paid monthly.

Location

Remote.

About us

Haiku Interactive Limited is a videogame development company founded in 2019, based in Edinburgh, Scotland. Haiku Interactive was originally a partnership founded in 2009 producing titles for the XBLIG marketplace on the Xbox 360. Titles released by the original partnership were Crate Expectations, AvaGlide, Space Police on Superhighway 9 and AvaGlide 2. The partnership was dissolved in 2011 but continued as a sole-trader releasing Dungeon Smash ~ Dark Isles in 2013. The sole-trader ceased trading in 2018. Now that sole-trader is back, devoting all their efforts to Dwerg Saga.
